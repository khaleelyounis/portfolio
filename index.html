A Little About Me

The idea of programming and what goes on behind the scenes has always been an interest of mine, originating from when I had just began to play video games. As I have grown up and seen a lot more of what the world has to offer, my curiousity evolved into a passion for wanting to create.

I love the idea of using the knowledge that I have accumulated to produce the backend to any project I have the privledge of being a part of. Server side code has become my new video game, as I enjoy every moment of taking a project from nothing to being fully deployed. Working alongside individuals with similar motivations and drive, is definitely something I charish and thrive in.

If I am not coding, you can catch me: at a hockey game (Go Ducks!), spending time with my family and friends, and of course playing a few video games. In life I believe there is a fine balance between work and play, but ultimately the most important idea to keep in mind is happiness. A quote I choose to follow goes a little like, "Happiness is only one step away, so take that step."
